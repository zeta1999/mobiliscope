About

Mobiliscope is a geovisualization platform to explore present population in cities over a 24 hour time period.
With the Mobiliscope, let's discover how cities, neighborhood social composition and urban segregation change around the clock.

Behind the Mobiliscope, there is a motivated team of geographers and geomaticians working in Géographie-cités laboratory (Paris, France) and also some supporting partners.

With the Mobiliscope licensed under the GNU GPLv3 license (a free, copyleft license), we want to take part to the open source community and open science.

In the actual version, cities geovizualisation has been optimized for Firefox, Chrome, Opera and IE Edge. Please check your browser.
