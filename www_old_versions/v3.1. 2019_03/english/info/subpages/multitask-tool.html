A multitask tool

Data about daily mobility are increasing but they may be difficult to handle and use.
Mobiliscope is an open and interactive platform intended to help large public explore cities and their temporal rythms.
Diverse audience may be concerned : Mobiliscope is all together a planning tool for policymakers, a scientific tool for researchers, and also a pedagogical tool for teachers and students.

And whatever your profile, you can also play with the Mobiliscope just for fun !
